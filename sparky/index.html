Our Vision

We shall create a system where food products are automatically discounted as they reach their expiry date, encouraging shoppers to reduce food waste. Additionally, beverages will be sold in glass bottles at a higher price (e.g., water at ₹70). When customers return the empty bottle, they receive a ₹50 refund, promoting a circular economy.

Dynamic Discounting Engine: Use IoT shelf sensors (e.g., Siemens SIMATIC) to monitor perishable products. Apply escalating discounts (10% → 70%) as expiry approaches. Push notifications alert app users with offers like: "Salmon: 30% off, expires in 24h!"

Real-World Reference: Walmart’s GenAI flags near-expiry items for markdowns/donations (Walmart Global Tech). Tech Stack: Azure IoT Edge + PySpark for data processing and real-time analytics.
